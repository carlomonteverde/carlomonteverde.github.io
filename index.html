BACKGROUND OF THE BUSINESS

ALLO'S Recyclable Paper Shop sell a different kinds of recyclable paper like Bags,Coin Bank,Flower Vase and Mini Basket. Established on August 2017 owned by Carlo P. Monteverde. This business is to increased paper recovery for recycling and to think the importance of recycling. It has a number of important benefits besides saving trees from being cut down

MISSION

To promote increased paper recovery for recycling by raising awareness about the importance of recycling, educating key stakeholders about the sucess of the voluntary paper recovery system, and providing tools and resources to help communities, business and schools start or improve paper recycling

GOALS AND OBLECTIVES

Be the preferred resource for paper recycling educational and program improvement materials and cooperation of individual faculty,staff, and students is essential to continue the recycling program's sucess.

VISION

* To reduce the amount of waste by promoting individual and corporate responsibility.
* To recover waste for its highest and best use while balancing rates and services.
* To transform our business from burying waste to utilizing waste as a resource.
